    • About Us

    Friend App is a platform for renting personal time and skills. Taking advantage of the prevailing environment of the sharing economy, Friend App is constantly innovating. While integrating the user’s personal time and skills and realizing the user’s personal value, we are committed to createcreating a healthy, professional and beautiful rental platform.


    We see ourselves as the National Broker

    Ingenious product positioning transforms personal spare time and limited talents and skills into a rental mode through integration into the platform, fully realizing personal value and making life more interesting.


    The Big Idea

    Users put their idle time to good use by renting their time and controlling their own hourly rates. Once there is a demand, the other user (renter) buys it. Users can use their free time to rent personal skills, play sports, cook together, have tea or coffee, and other social activities etc.


    Our Strategies

    Based on in-depth insights into the leasing market trends and the social habits of people, the Friend App introduces 2 concepts:


    1. New Four Modernization Concepts: The four concepts of innovation, fashion, personalization and socialization will lead the company’s positioning development, product positioning and user service system.

    2. Three Major Upgrade Policies: While adhering to the New Four Modernizations, Friend App also actively learns the essence of the success of internet companies, and keeps up with the times and continuously upgrades from product functions, information security, and user experience
